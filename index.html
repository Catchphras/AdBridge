The Process

From discovery to results — in five clear steps.

Every campaign follows a structured workflow built for quality and transparency.

01

Discovery

We reach out via WhatsApp or meet in person to understand your promotional needs, target audience, and goals — no jargon, just a real conversation.

02

Briefing

You complete our structured intake form: your brand, product, target customer, visual preferences, key messages, timeline, and package of interest.

03

Proposal

Our Account Manager presents a tailored package recommendation with a clear scope of exactly what you'll receive — no surprises.

04

Production & Review

Our Creative Director, Graphic Designer, Copywriter, and Video Producer execute your campaign. All work passes a two-stage quality review before it reaches you.

05

Delivery & Reporting

We publish your campaign and deliver a performance report covering reach, impressions, engagement, and where measurable — sales attributed to the campaign.
